About Us

Chapel for His Deliverance Ministries is a Christ-centred church committed to prayer, discipleship, and practical community care. We exist to see people restored, families strengthened, and communities transformed by the Gospel.

Mission: We aim to be a foundation and resource for the positive development and growth of the individual and community life. We will work alongside all groups within the community, ensuring a welcoming and safe environment where all individuals are valued, irrespective of age, gender, ethnic origin, sexual orientation, disability, appearance or marital status.
Vision: A praying, generous, multi-generational church that impacts our city.
Core Values: Prayer • Word • Compassion • Integrity • Excellence
